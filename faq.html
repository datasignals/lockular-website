FAQ's

Why should I choose Lockular?

Because you want military-grade data protection and traceability. Because you want data integrity that's unbelievably easy-to-use. Because you want to be able to permission use of your app data by GenAI.

How does Lockular work?

First, we encrypt your data with military-grade technology. Then we slice and dice it into pieces, before storing your data in secure depots all over the world. Only you can retrieve the pieces and put them back together. This keeps your data private and secure.

The security protocol provides the hooks for an immutable and trustless audit of access.

Why do you say it's a trustless audit?

The audit is NOT controlled by a single entity that has to be trusted. Instead the audit is blockchain based and under the control of a consensus mechanism. Consensus mechanisms include prrof of work, proof of stake, and proof of authority.

How easy is it to use?

We pride ourselves on keeping our technology ultra-easy to deploy as well as ultra-simple to understand. All you need to do is plug into our cloud drive and pay. That's it.

Do you roll your own cryptography?

Absolutely not. We leverage standard techniques from Shamir Secret Sharing to Affine transforms to slice the data. We use standard blockchain techniques to lay down the audit, alongside well published techniques for fast ZKPs. Throughout we use secure communication protocols and exercise strong penetration and vulnerability testing.

What's interesting about Lockular's core tech then?

Lockular can be integrated as a filesystem, built entirely on blockchain technology. Our data storage protocol ensures that audit checkpoints are immutable. Since the blockchain autonomously manages the protocol, it guarantees that these audits cannot be tampered with.

How secure is ultra-secure?

No one has ever cracked our security. A leading military unit with an infinite budget might just have a chance. But if someone like that really wants your data, we think you have bigger problems to worry about.

What happens if I sign up and get hacked?

You might get hacked, but the data you store with us won't. Guaranteed.
