Dr Ron Black

Chairman

Ron is currently the CEO of Codasip, a German firm that specialises in tools for accelerating the design of custom RISC-V chips. Previously, he has served as CEO at Imagination Technologies, Rambus, UPEK, and Wavecom. He has also held leadership positions at Lucent, Gemplus, IBM, and Motorola.

Gary Mawdsley MSc

CEO

Gary was responsible for establishing the UK's first internet banking system to critical acclaim in the late 90s. He went on to develop ideas in the area of microservices, service orchestration and data privacy across segments from education to financial services. The holder of two patents in service assembly and data privacy, Gary's current work is focused in protecting data from the infrastructure both at rest and when data is in flight, with particular reference to homomorphic encrypted data and signals based AI.

Richard Sunderland

CMO

As CEO founder of consultancy Heavenly Group, Richard has advised technology companies in the US and UK over a number of years, particularly in the semiconductor and data security space. Richard is a specialist in corporate and product positioning, go-to-market strategy and partnerships.
