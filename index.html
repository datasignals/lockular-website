    We are Lockular

    Lockular is a premier data security firm based in the UK, committed to delivering advanced, user-friendly security solutions that ensure data integrity across diverse industries. We cater to organisations of all sizes, guaranteeing that their most crucial asset—data—is secured to the highest standards.

    Our specialisation is deeply entrenched in Supply Chain Provenance. Lockular excels in developing systems that provide undeniable proof of the history and integrity of both physical and electronic products from their origin to the end-user. Our provenance tracking expertise enables businesses to authenticate and manage the custody of their products, effectively reducing fraud, increasing transparency, and strengthening trust throughout the supply chain.

    Lockular offers a robust Supply Chain Provenance suite, consisting of six main components supported by two proprietary, patent-protected technologies. These components are designed to be cloud-native and integrate seamlessly into existing infrastructures. The solution is optimised for both public and private cloud environments, with Kubernetes as a crucial requirement.

    We utilise cutting-edge technologies to monitor the transmission and consumption of goods. Our systems integrate flawlessly with sensors and other IoT devices across the supply chain, ensuring that all data related to product movement and handling is securely captured, transmitted, and managed. This not only enhances security but also provides vital insights into the logistics and management of sensitive materials.
