    We are Lockular

We cater to organisations of all sizes, guaranteeing that their most crucial asset—data—is secured to the highest standards.

Lockular excels in developing systems that provide undeniable proof of the history and integrity of both physical and electronic products from their origin to the end-user. Our provenance tracking expertise enables businesses to authenticate and manage the custody of their products, effectively reducing fraud, increasing transparency, and strengthening trust throughout the supply chain.

These components are designed to be cloud-native and integrate seamlessly into existing infrastructures. The solution is optimised for both public and private cloud environments, with Kubernetes as a crucial requirement.

    We utilise cutting-edge technologies to monitor the transmission and consumption of goods. Our systems integrate flawlessly with sensors and other IoT devices across the supply chain, ensuring that all data related to product movement and handling is securely captured, transmitted, and managed. This not only enhances security but also provides vital insights into the logistics and management of sensitive materials.

    Organisations frequently select specific components of the Lockular suite to address immediate challenges, allowing for a customised approach to bolster their supply chain security. For concerns requiring military-grade security, Lockular's components are especially effective in meeting ITAR and CMMC defense standards, particularly in the irrefutable tracking of team structures across organisations.
  • Silicon Chip Use Case

    Silicon Chip Use Case

    Overview:

    Provenance suite has been successfully implemented in the specialised area of Silicon Chip Design and Manufacturing, specifically customised for Codasip, a leader in tailored RISC-V design tools. This scenario utilises three main components from the Lockular Supply Chain Provenance suite: SecureWorking, Supply Chain Actor Management (SCA), and the NFT Marketplace platform.

    Supply Chain Actor Management (SCA): SCA is critical in shaping and overseeing the cooperative relationships among organisations involved in the chip design and manufacturing processes. Using Smart contract technology, SCA sets strict protocols that dictate how these organisations interact, collaborate, and exchange design information. This organised approach ensures a high level of precision and compliance throughout the collaborative efforts.

    SecureWorking: SecureWorking is used to offer chip designers a cloud-based Integrated Development Environment (IDE) powered by Microsoft Visual Studio Code, specifically tailored for Codasip's chip design toolchain. The cloud setup is securely isolated, supported by the Lockular Provenance Tracking filesystem, which ensures comprehensive tracking and security of all activities and design data within the environment. Access to this platform is tightly controlled by Codasip-specific smart contracts managed by SCA, ensuring that only authorised personnel can access the critical design tools and data and in turn all activity recorded.

    Provenance Tracking and Security: Every aspect of the designs produced within SecureWorking is continuously monitored and documented on a dedicated Polkadot parachain (blockchain) by Lockular's Provenance Tracking filesystem. This blockchain-based monitoring system provides a permanent record of all design actions, modifications, and accesses, significantly enhancing the security and traceability of the design process. This detailed monitoring is essential for protecting intellectual property and maintaining the integrity of the design process in the highly competitive and secretive chip design sector. Provenance records and reports are a significant component in fulfilling the requirement of the US ITAR standard used across the defense sector.

    Integration with NFT Marketplace: The NFT Marketplace is utilised to tokenise design prototypes or intellectual property, facilitating secure and verifiable transactions within the design ecosystem. For Codasip, this enables the sharing of common chip IP block definitions among teams within the partner network. IP Blocks form the basis of a larger single chip design.

    Benefit: The deployment of Lockular's Provenance suite has equipped Codasip with a secure, transparent, and efficient environment for their chip design tooling. This integration has not only bolstered the security and integrity of the design process but also enhanced collaboration and compliance, leading to heightened productivity and innovation in chip design platform. Codasip has named this integration Secure Studio in the Cloud (SSitC) and is collaborating with Lockular to market the Provenance Supply Chain for RISC-V design services.

  • Pharmaceuticals Use Case

    Pharmaceuticals Use Case

    Overview:

    This use case is currently being developed in collaboration with a partner who approached Lockular with the concept. The partner will have exclusive rights to sell the solution on a global scale, with support from Lockular.

    Lockular's Provenance suite is being tailored to address the stringent requirements of the Falsified Medicines Directive (FMD) in the pharmaceutical industry. Fundamentally this means the creation of a domain specific contract that drives the behaviour of Lockular's Smart Contract Actor Management system. Data is not stored on chain but inside of Lockular's Provenance tracking filesystem.

    This use case leverages key components from the Lockular Supply Chain Provenance suite to ensure compliance with the FMD's delegated regulation, which mandates the verification of medicinal products' authenticity through a decommissioning process. This process involves scanning a unique 2D barcode assigned to every manufactured medicinal product pack at various levels: the individual item, a package of items, and a pallet of packages.

    Workflow: The typical workflow starts with the manufacture of the medicinal products, where each unit is assigned a unique 2D barcode. These units are then packaged and palletised for distribution. The workflow progresses as follows:

    1. 1. Manufacture: Medicinal products are manufactured and each unit is assigned a unique 2D barcode. Units are then packaged into larger groups and palletized.

    2. 2. Shipping to Wholesaler: The pallets are shipped to wholesalers, where the integrity and authenticity of the barcodes are verified and logged via the Supply Chain Proveannce solution.

    3. 3. Wholesaler to Customer: Wholesalers distribute the pallets or break them down into smaller packages to ship to customers, typically hospitals.

    4. 4. Hospital to Patient: Within the hospital, medical practitioners issue the medicinal products to patients. Each issued unit's barcode is scanned to verify its authenticity.

    5. 5. Decommissioning: Once a medicinal product is issued to a patient, its barcode is decommissioned, marking it as used in the system to prevent reuse or falsification.

    Supply Chain Tracking and Compliance: Throughout the supply chain, the assembly of packages and pallets is meticulously tracked at the manufacturing stage. As pallets are shipped and subsequently broken down by wholesalers, each step is recorded. This tracking continues until the final delivery to the patient. Lockular's Provenance Tracking system plays a crucial role in this process:

    1. 1. Provenance Tracking and Security: The entire journey of each medicinal product, from manufacturing to patient delivery, is continuously monitored and recorded on a dedicated blockchain system via Lockular's Provenance Tracking filesystem. This ensures an immutable record of all transactions, changes, and decommissioning actions, significantly enhancing the security, traceability, and compliance of the distribution process.

    2. 2. Integration with Regulatory Requirements: The system is designed to comply with the FMD regulations by ensuring that all medicinal products can be authenticated and decommissioned according to legal requirements. This helps prevent the distribution of falsified medicines and ensures patient safety.

    Goal: The implementation of Lockular's Provenance suite will provides a secure, transparent, and compliant environment for the manufacturing and distribution of medicinal products. This not only enhances the security and integrity of the medicinal supply chain but also ensures full compliance with the FMD regulations, leading to increased trust and safety in pharmaceutical distribution.

  • Provenance Tracking for Susan Shaw Handbags

    Provenance Tracking for Susan Shaw Handbags

    Overview:

    The process begins at the manufacturing stage where each handbag is treated with SmartWater, a unique identifier that marks the material of the handbag. This identifier is linked to an NFT, establishing the handbag's initial provenance record. here's the supply chain flows outlined:

    1. 1. Manufacture: Each Susan Shaw handbag is sprayed with SmartWater at the point of manufacture, providing a unique identifier that is associated with an NFT to create a provenance record.

    2. 2. Initial Purchase: Upon purchase, the NFT is transferred to the buyer, updating the ownership in the provenance record.

    3. 3. Resale Process:

    1.    a. The seller sends the handbag to the service provider.

    2.    b. The service provider verifies the handbag's authenticity by checking the SmartWater mark.

    3.    c. The handbag's condition is assessed.

    4.    d. The provenance record is updated, and the handbag is dispatched to the new owner as per the updated record.

    For legacy handbags [pre-SmartWater] the app allows handbags to be sent in for service and the authenticity assessed with the option of retrospective SmartWater based provenance registration.

    Mobile App Integration: The mobile app serves as a platform for transactions, where users can buy, sell, or request restoration of handbags. It integrates a Polkadot.js wallet, enabling seamless transfers of NFTs associated with each handbag. This ensures that every transaction is recorded and the handbag's provenance is maintained and accessible.

    Benefits: This use case leverages Lockular's Provenance suite to provide a secure, transparent, and reliable system for tracking the lifecycle of luxury handbags. By ensuring the authenticity and condition of each handbag through a detailed provenance record, the system not only enhances consumer trust but also supports the luxury market in maintaining the value and integrity of high-end products.
