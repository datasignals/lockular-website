What We Do

Data security is a problem. Today, breaches are so common that the media reports them daily. At Lockular, we help companies keep their data safe.

Keep Data Safe

No business should use the cloud without proper data protection. It’s simply too risky. And an unnecessary risk too. Our data protection solutions are so easy-to-use, any business can take charge of their data security. And while it’s impossible to eliminate every risk, our data solutions come close because they’re made with the best defensive technology out there. We help businesses share, store and communicate in the cloud in safety.

Our clients are in the connected devices space, where the IoT enables machines to communicate. Or SMEs that’s recently moved your servers to the cloud. We help keep private stuff secure.

Ultra Simple, Secure Solutions

We encrypt your data with military-grade technology. Then we slice and dice it into impossibly tiny bits, before storing it in secure depots all over the world. Only you can retrieve the bits and put them back together. This keeps your data private and secure.
